Meny

Welcome to Meny, the beautifully simple menu bar app for taking notes and writing in markdown. With Meny, you can easily create and organize your thoughts, ideas, and to-dos in a distraction-free environment. Whether you're a student, a writer, or just need a place to jot down your daily musings, Meny makes it easy to write in markdown and see your formatted text in real-time.
